FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How can I be sure that my vote and information are safe on this platform?
Your data and participation are secured on this platform. The online voting system is built on the highest security protocol and standards. It also uses advanced cryptographic methods to protect user security and privacy.
How long does it take for my vote to be counted?
This is an online voting system that speeds up the ballot counting process and returns voting instantly after they are submitted. Once you finish casting your vote, it is immediately processed, tabulated, and added to the final tally.
How can I receive election date reminders?
The system will provide reminders about upcoming election dates directly to the user. These reminders can be set up via email and dashboard. This online voting system also allows you to set a calendar view of all upcoming elections, which can be synced to your personal device.
Would my vote be kept private and secure?
Yes, your vote will be kept secret and secured on the online voting system. The system is designed to protect the integrity of the vote and ensure that the results are accurate and untampered with.
