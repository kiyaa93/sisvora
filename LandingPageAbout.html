ABOUT US

SISVORA is a Digital Voting System designed for the OSIS presidential election. It helps schools conduct fair and transparent elections by providing modern and reliable results. We aim to make student voting simple, secure, and accessible for everyone.

OUR STRENGTHS

Increased Voter Turnout

Encourage more participation by making it easy and convenient for everyone to vote.

Accurate Vote Records

With automatic vote-tabulation, there is no error in the counting method and can be verified.

Cost-Effective

Digital elections cut costs. No papers to be printed, and only a few equipment needed.

Guaranteed Security

The student's identity and vote data are kept extremely secure compared with paper-based methods.
